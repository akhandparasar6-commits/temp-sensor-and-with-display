
## 🔌 Circuit Connections

### 1️⃣ DS18B20 Temperature Sensor
- VCC → 3.3V  
- GND → GND  
- DATA → GPIO 5  
- Use 4.7kΩ pull-up resistor between VCC and DATA  

### 2️⃣ OLED Display (SSD1306 - I2C)
- VCC → 3.3V  
- GND → GND  
- SDA → GPIO 21  
- SCL → GPIO 22  
- I2C Address → 0x3C  

### 3️⃣ Buzzer
- Positive → GPIO 25  
- Negative → GND
